Place your licensed Museo Sans Cyrl font files in this folder.

Recommended filenames:
- MuseoSansCyrl-Regular.woff2
- MuseoSansCyrl-Regular.woff
- MuseoSansCyrl-Bold.woff2
- MuseoSansCyrl-Bold.woff

Then, in styles/style.css, uncomment the @font-face blocks and ensure the paths match.

Note: Museo Sans Cyrillic is a commercial font. Make sure you have a valid license from the foundry (exljbris) or an authorized distributor before embedding.
